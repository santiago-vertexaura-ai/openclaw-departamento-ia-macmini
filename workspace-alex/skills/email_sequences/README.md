## Objetivo
Disenar secuencias de email story-based usando framework de Russell Brunson.

## Proceso
1. Consultar vault: brunson-story-selling, email-sequence patterns
2. Definir la narrativa: Character > Desire > Wall > Epiphany > Plan > Conflict > Achievement > Transformation
3. Estructurar 5-7 emails con arco narrativo
4. Cada email: subject line, preview text, body, CTA
5. Timing entre emails

## Output esperado
- 5-7 emails completos
- Subject lines A/B testeable
- CTAs claros por email
- Timing recomendado
- Metricas objetivo (open rate, click rate)
